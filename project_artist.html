Artist Statement

People have their reasons for why they like art and for me, I like it because it provides a fun visual for the words and stories I want to tell. I especially love to put some sort of meaning behind each of my pieces whether it’s something personal to myself or the person I drew it for. I was always rather sensitive both to my own emotions and others’ growing up, but I could never find the right way to express those feelings in words, so I drew them. If I was trying to cheer a friend up, I’d draw them a card with something they loved on it like their pet or their favorite character from a show. It was my way of saying “I hope you feel better”, but with a more personal feel that they could hold in their own hands.

Even if I’m not trying to put some sort of sentimental meaning to my works, the one thing that always stays consistent is how I like to have some sort of bright color present. Even if the piece is mostly a neutral color like black or gray I like to sneak in some sort of color to balance it. In the end, I just love looking at pretty colors.
